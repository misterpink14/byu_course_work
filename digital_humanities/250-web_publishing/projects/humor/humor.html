What is Humor?

Humor to me

To me, humor is more than just laughter. Humor offers a release from stress. Humor keeps my optomistic about what is coming up next. Instead of focusing on the next difficult task before me, I can brush it off while joking around with my wife or friends. Then, when I have to get something time, I am less stressed. This is because I haven't been spending my time focused on it or worried about it, I just handle it when it comes up.
