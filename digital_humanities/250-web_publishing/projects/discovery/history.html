Brief History

Ruby is 22 years old. It's inventor was Yukihiro Matsumoto, known as "Matz." Ruby's birth was in Japan in 1993. Version 1.0 was released in December 1996. Currently, it is on version 2.1.2. It is the only programming language invented by a member of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints.
