Supernatural

Two brothers, Sam and Dean Winchester, fight demons (which I associate with fire) and other evil supernatural forces while they travel the country in their black 1967 Chevy Impala. This car turns out to be the most important object of the series. Sam and Dean's brotherly love turns out to be the most powerful supernatural force of the series.

Scrubs

John Dorian, often referred to as J.D., starts as a first year intern at Sacred Heart Hospital (their logo uses red and blue) and grows to become a doctor. Hidden behind the fun and humor lies life lessons of morality friendship and love. J.D. relies on his relationships throughout the entire series. Scrubs are often colored teal and turquoise.

Gotham

Step into the world of Gotham before Batman and his foes. Teenage Bruce learns of the corruption in the city and within his family's company. Young adult Gordon fights to rid Gotham of the corruption that is infecting his city. The future villains of the already dark and gloomy Gotham begin to emerge and their stories begin.

Daredevil

Matt Murdock is the Devil of Hell's Kitchen, dubbed Daredevil. As a recent Law School graduate, he fights corruption of Hell's Kitchen through the law by day. By night, he fights corruption as a vigilante in his dark red, almost purple, suit. As he struggles to balance his double life, he fights those threatening the citizens of his hometown.
