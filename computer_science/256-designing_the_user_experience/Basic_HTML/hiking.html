Hiking

Equipment: hiking shoes, water, snacks, backpack
Weather: best in cloudy sky and comfortable tempurature

The Experience

Hiking is a fairly intense adventure walk, usually with the goal of reaching a beatiful view of some sort. Often there are steep uphill and downhill spells while hiking.

Hiking is about seeing parts of nature that you wouldn't normally be able to see. Hiking is rigorous, but work the effort.
