Camping

Equipment: tent and sleeping bag -or- rv and bedding, flashlight, matches, firewood, food
Weather: best with clear skies, although poor weather makes for good stories

The Experience

Camping is enjoying the outdoors during an overnight, or several, adventure. Hiking and camping are often integrated with eachother.

Camping is all about the experience. Some campers enjoy hiking to their campsite. Some campers enjoy day hiking.
